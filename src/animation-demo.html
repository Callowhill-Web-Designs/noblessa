How to Use

Available Classes:

  • .sl-fade-in-up - Fades in from bottom to top
  • .sl-fade-in-down - Fades in from top to bottom
  • .sl-fade-in-left - Fades in from right to left
  • .sl-fade-in-right - Fades in from left to right

Usage Examples:
