Our Legacy

transforming living spaces & lives

noblessa has been a brand of excellence for high-end kitchens since 1996. For almost 30 years, our premium kitchens are made in our state-of-the-art factories located in Germany, and overseen by expert craftsmen. From Dubai to Beirut, Los Angeles, Istanbul, and Prague, we have set the benchmark for high-end kitchen design around the world. The perfection of a noblessa kitchen can be seen in the smallest details. More than a brand, it’s a way of life. We are committed to designing living spaces where elegance and harmony reign supreme – by taking our inspiration from you and your desires. That is who we are.
