Meet Stephen & Michael

Just outside of Los Angeles, interior designer Stephen Lazar and his lifelong partner and husband, Michael Grazer, welcome us into their home. He is here to show us his noblessa kitchen transformation and to share his insights on why he chose to design his custom kitchen with noblessa.

The Lazar home’s freshly remodeled kitchen is inspired by the upscale formality of European-designed living spaces. This modern California kitchen integrates sharp, clean lines with elements that speak directly to the owner’s eclectic personal style. The result? A custom kitchen with character, a kitchen that represents Stephen & Michael in a physical form – uniquely sophisticated, with cosmopolitan appeal.

Step Inside the Lazar Home

The Lazar kitchen is a true reflection of Stephen and Michael’s vision: refined, personal, and unapologetically modern. Inspired by European design, the space balances clean architectural lines with rich textures and curated details that speak to their eclectic style. From the sleek cabinetry to the sculptural lighting, every element feels intentional, crafted not just for function, but as an extension of how the Lazars live, entertain, and express themselves.
